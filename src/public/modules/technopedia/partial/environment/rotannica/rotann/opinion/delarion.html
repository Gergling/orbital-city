The {{race}} are extremely well travelled for a non-mechanical civilisation of their age. They interact with space and time in a way previously unprecedented. This perception has resulted in advanced space-travel technology.

Having studied our mathematics, they have communicated our universe to have an 'unorthodox shape'. However, due to the origins of their perception, their mathematical model is different, and the shape has symmetry and order.

This is probably why their perception of their journey was 'to move down a level', while our perception of their journey appears to be from one side of the universe to the other.
